Rescuing Animals to Rescue People

What is Wellness Ranch Therapy?

Wellness Ranch Therapy is a sanctuary where mental health meets nature. But how does it work?

"Horses act as mirrors to our emotions. They help trauma survivors process feelings without words. When a horse accepts you, you learn to accept yourself."

For a veteran with PTSD or a child with anxiety, traditional talk therapy can be intimidating. Here, under the open sky, healing happens naturally.

Where Your Money Goes

Animal therapy is an alternative and experimental field that is difficult to sustain for profit. We offer the promise that 100% of your support goes directly to the herd.

  • Nutrition & Health

    Quality timothy hay, grain, and specialized supplements for our senior animals.

  • Professional Care

    Routine farrier visits for hoof health and regular veterinary check-ups.

  • Subsidized Therapy

    Your support lowers the cost of sessions for families in crisis and veterans.
